About Dr. Jonathan Kramer

Home / About

Dr. Jonathan Kramer, MD

Board-Certified Plastic Surgeon

Dr. Jonathan Kramer is a board-certified plastic surgeon dedicated to helping patients in Boise and the Treasure Valley achieve beautiful transformations. With extensive training and a passion for personalized care, Dr. Kramer is known for creating natural-looking results that enhance confidence and quality of life.

Dr. Kramer attended Vanderbilt University, majoring in Molecular Biology. He received a Bachelor of Arts Degree and earned a Masters Degree, which included a guest on a Fulbright grant. He continued his diverse interests by studying the genetic basis of cancers with a joint research position between Emory University and the Centers for Disease Control.

He then earned his Medical Degree at Emory University in Atlanta, Georgia, followed by completion of a five-year residency in general surgery at the Phoenix Integrated Surgical Residency Program, where he was awarded the "Outstanding Surgical Intern" in his class. He went on to complete a two-year plastic surgery fellowship at the University of Colorado Health Sciences Center in Denver.

After practicing several years in Phoenix, AZ, he subsequently relocated his practice to the Boise area. Today, Dr. Kramer specializes in breast augmentation, facelifts, body contouring, and a comprehensive range of plastic surgery procedures for patients throughout Idaho.
