About Dr. Aldo Guerra

Home / About

Dr. Aldo Guerra, MD, FACS

Board-Certified Plastic Surgeon

With over 15 years of experience and thousands of successful procedures, Dr. Aldo Guerra has established himself as one of Scottsdale' most respected plastic surgeons, known for his meticulous technique and natural-looking results.

Dr. Aldo Guerra's approach to plastic surgery combines surgical precision with an artistic eye, ensuring each patient receives personalized care tailored to their unique anatomy and aesthetic goals. He believes that the best results are those that enhance your natural beauty while looking completely natural.

After completing his medical degree at Harvard Medical School, Dr. Aldo Guerra pursued rigorous training in general surgery at Johns Hopkins Hospital before completing his plastic surgery fellowship at UCLA Medical Center. This extensive training provided him with a comprehensive foundation in both reconstructive and aesthetic surgery.

Today, Dr. Aldo Guerra specializes in breast augmentation, facelifts, rhinoplasty, body contouring, and mommy makeovers. He is committed to staying at the forefront of plastic surgery innovations, regularly attending conferences and incorporating the latest techniques to provide his patients with the safest, most effective procedures available.
