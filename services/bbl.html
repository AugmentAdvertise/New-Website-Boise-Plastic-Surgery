Achieve Natural Curves with Brazilian Butt Lift in Scottsdale

The Brazilian Butt Lift (BBL) enhances your buttocks using your own natural fat, creating fuller, rounder, and more lifted curves while simultaneously contouring the areas from which fat is harvested. Dr. Aldo Guerra combines artistic vision with surgical expertise to create beautiful, proportionate results that look and feel natural.

Unlike buttock implants, the BBL uses fat transfer to augment the buttocks, providing a natural look and feel. The procedure offers a dual benefit: liposuction removes unwanted fat from areas like the abdomen, flanks, and thighs, while that same fat is purified and injected into the buttocks to create your desired shape.

Benefits of Brazilian Butt Lift

  • Natural-looking, natural-feeling results using your own fat
  • Dual benefit: body contouring through liposuction plus buttock enhancement
  • Fuller, rounder, more lifted buttocks
  • Improved waist-to-hip ratio and curves
  • No implants or foreign materials
  • Smoother contours in donor areas
  • Long-lasting results

The BBL Procedure

Step 1: Liposuction

Fat is harvested through liposuction from donor areas such as the abdomen, flanks (love handles), lower back, and thighs. This step contours these areas while collecting the fat needed for transfer.

Step 2: Fat Processing

The harvested fat is carefully processed and purified to separate healthy fat cells from blood, oil, and other fluids. Only the highest-quality fat cells are used for transfer.

Step 3: Fat Injection

The purified fat is strategically injected into the buttocks at various depths and locations to create the desired shape, volume, and projection. Dr. Aldo Guerra uses advanced techniques to maximize fat survival and create natural-looking results.

Am I a Candidate for a Brazilian Butt Lift?

You may be an ideal candidate for a BBL if you:

  • Desire a fuller, rounder, more lifted buttocks
  • Have enough excess fat in donor areas for transfer
  • Want natural-looking results without implants
  • Are at a stable, healthy weight
  • Are a non-smoker or willing to quit
  • Have realistic expectations about the results

Patients who are very thin may not have enough donor fat for a BBL and may need to consider buttock implants or a combination approach.

Safety First

Dr. Aldo Guerra prioritizes patient safety and follows all protocols established by the American Society of Plastic Surgeons for BBL procedures. This includes:

  • Injecting fat only into the subcutaneous space (above the muscle)
  • Using ultrasound guidance when appropriate
  • Limiting the volume of fat transferred
  • Performing the procedure in an accredited surgical facility

Recovery & Results

Recovery from a BBL requires special attention to protect the transferred fat. You'll need to avoid sitting directly on your buttocks for 2-3 weeks and wear a compression garment for several weeks.

Recovery Timeline:

  • Week 1-2: Rest, avoid sitting on buttocks, wear compression garment
  • Week 2-3: Use a BBL pillow when sitting, continue compression
  • Week 3-4: Return to work (with modified sitting), light activities
  • Week 6-8: Resume most normal activities
  • Month 3: Resume exercise, final results emerging

Expect some initial swelling and that some of the transferred fat will be absorbed. Typically 60-80% of the transferred fat survives long-term. Final results are visible at 3-6 months and are long-lasting with stable weight.
