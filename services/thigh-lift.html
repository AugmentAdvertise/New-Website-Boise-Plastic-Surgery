Thigh Lift

Home / Body / Thigh Lift

Achieve Smoother, More Contoured Thighs in Scottsdale

A thigh lift removes excess skin and fat from the inner and/or outer thighs, creating smoother, more proportionate leg contours. Dr. Aldo Guerra uses precise surgical techniques to eliminate sagging skin and give you thighs you'll feel confident showing off.

Sagging skin on the thighs is a common concern, particularly after significant weight loss or as a natural part of aging. The inner thighs are especially prone to skin laxity, and no amount of exercise can tighten loose skin. A thigh lift provides a solution, removing excess skin and creating smooth, youthful leg contours.

Benefits of Thigh Lift Surgery

  • Elimination of excess, sagging skin on the thighs
  • Smoother, more toned thigh appearance
  • Improved leg proportions
  • Reduced chafing and skin irritation
  • Greater confidence in shorts, swimwear, and fitted clothing
  • Long-lasting results

Types of Thigh Lifts

Inner (Medial) Thigh Lift

The most common approach, addressing loose skin on the inner thighs. The incision is placed in the groin crease, extending down the inner thigh as needed. This technique is ideal for patients with mild to moderate skin laxity.

Vertical Thigh Lift

For patients with significant skin excess extending down the inner thigh, a vertical incision is made from the groin to the knee. This allows for maximum skin removal but results in a longer scar.

Outer (Lateral) Thigh Lift

Addresses loose skin on the outer thighs and is typically performed as part of a lower body lift. The incision extends from the groin around to the buttock crease.

Spiral Thigh Lift

A comprehensive approach that addresses the inner, outer, front, and back of the thigh through a combination of incisions. Often performed for patients after massive weight loss.

Am I a Candidate for a Thigh Lift?

You may be an ideal candidate for a thigh lift if you have:

  • Loose, sagging skin on the inner or outer thighs
  • Excess skin after significant weight loss
  • Age-related skin laxity in the thighs
  • Skin that doesn't respond to diet and exercise
  • Chafing or irritation from excess thigh skin
  • Self-consciousness about your thigh appearance

Ideal candidates are at a stable weight, non-smokers, and have realistic expectations. Dr. Aldo Guerra will evaluate your skin quality and recommend the best approach during your consultation.

The Consultation Process

Your thigh lift journey begins with a comprehensive consultation. During this appointment, you will:

  • Discuss your concerns and aesthetic goals
  • Review your medical history and weight loss history
  • Undergo an examination of your thighs
  • Learn about the surgical technique recommended for you
  • View before and after photos of similar patients
  • Discuss scarring and recovery expectations

Recovery & Results

After surgery, you'll wear compression garments to minimize swelling and support healing. Drains may be placed temporarily to prevent fluid accumulation. Walking will be limited initially to protect the incisions.

Recovery Timeline:

  • Week 1: Rest, limited walking, wear compression garments
  • Week 2: Gradually increase walking, drains typically removed
  • Week 3: Return to desk work, light activities
  • Week 6-8: Resume most normal activities and exercise
  • Month 3: Full recovery, final results emerging

Results are immediately visible and continue to improve over 3-6 months as swelling subsides. Thigh lift results are long-lasting with stable weight maintenance.
