Complete Your Weight Loss Journey with Body Lift Surgery in Scottsdale

After achieving significant weight loss, many patients are left with excess, sagging skin that no amount of diet or exercise can address. A body lift removes this excess skin from the abdomen, back, buttocks, and thighs, helping you achieve the toned, contoured body you've worked so hard for.

Massive weight loss—whether through bariatric surgery, diet, or exercise—is a tremendous accomplishment. However, the remaining loose skin can cause physical discomfort, hygiene issues, and emotional distress. A body lift addresses these concerns comprehensively, completing your transformation and allowing you to fully enjoy your weight loss success.

Benefits of Body Lift Surgery

  • Removal of excess, hanging skin from multiple body areas
  • Improved body contour and shape
  • Relief from skin irritation, rashes, and infections
  • Enhanced ability to exercise comfortably
  • Better-fitting clothing
  • Improved hygiene
  • Increased confidence and quality of life

Types of Body Lifts

Lower Body Lift (Belt Lipectomy)

The most comprehensive option, addressing the abdomen, outer thighs, buttocks, and lower back in a single procedure. The incision extends completely around the body at the beltline, allowing for 360-degree contouring.

Upper Body Lift

Addresses excess skin on the upper back, chest, and upper arms. Often combined with a breast lift for comprehensive upper body rejuvenation.

Extended Tummy Tuck

A modified approach that addresses the abdomen and extends to the hips and lower back, suitable for patients with more localized excess skin.

Am I a Candidate for a Body Lift?

You may be an ideal candidate for a body lift if you:

  • Have lost a significant amount of weight (typically 100+ pounds)
  • Have maintained a stable weight for at least 6 months
  • Have excess, sagging skin that causes discomfort or hygiene issues
  • Are in good overall health
  • Do not smoke or are willing to quit
  • Have realistic expectations about the results

The Consultation Process

Your body lift journey begins with a comprehensive consultation. During this appointment, you will:

  • Discuss your weight loss journey and goals
  • Review your medical history and nutritional status
  • Undergo a physical examination to assess excess skin
  • Learn about the surgical approach recommended for you
  • Discuss whether a staged approach would be beneficial
  • View before and after photos of similar patients
  • Discuss insurance coverage options if applicable

Surgical Approach

Body lift surgery is typically performed under general anesthesia and may take 4-7 hours depending on the extent of the procedure. For comprehensive body contouring, Dr. Aldo Guerra may recommend a staged approach with procedures performed 3-6 months apart.

The lower body lift incision extends around the entire body at the beltline. Through this incision, excess skin and fat are removed, underlying tissues are tightened, and the remaining skin is repositioned for optimal contour.

Recovery & Results

Body lift recovery is more extensive than other procedures due to the comprehensive nature of the surgery. You'll have drains in place for 1-2 weeks and will need to wear a compression garment for several weeks.

Recovery Timeline:

  • Week 1-2: Rest at home, limited mobility, pain management
  • Week 2-3: Drains removed, increased mobility
  • Week 3-4: Light activities, may return to desk work
  • Week 6-8: Resume most normal activities
  • Month 3-6: Resume strenuous exercise, final results emerging

Results are immediately visible and continue to improve over 6-12 months as swelling resolves and tissues settle. Body lift results are long-lasting with a stable weight.
