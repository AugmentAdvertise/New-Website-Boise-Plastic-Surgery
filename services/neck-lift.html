Neck Lift

Home / Face / Neck Lift

Restore a Youthful, Defined Neck Profile in Boise

A neck lift, also known as platysmaplasty, addresses the visible signs of aging in the neck and jawline area. Dr. Jonathan Kramer combines surgical expertise with artistic precision to eliminate sagging skin, tighten neck muscles, and create a smooth, youthful contour that beautifully frames your face.

The neck is often one of the first areas to show signs of aging. Factors such as genetics, sun exposure, and natural loss of skin elasticity can lead to loose skin, vertical neck bands, excess fat under the chin, and the development of jowls. A neck lift effectively addresses these concerns, restoring definition to your profile.

Benefits of Neck Lift Surgery

  • Elimination of "turkey neck" and loose, sagging skin
  • Smoothing of vertical neck bands (platysmal bands)
  • Reduction of jowls along the jawline
  • Removal of excess fat under the chin
  • Creation of a more defined jawline and neck angle
  • More youthful, refreshed appearance
  • Long-lasting results that can last 10-15 years

Am I a Candidate for a Neck Lift?

You may be an ideal candidate for a neck lift if you experience:

  • Loose, sagging skin on the neck
  • Visible vertical bands in the neck
  • Excess fat under the chin (double chin)
  • Loss of definition in the jawline
  • Jowls that have developed along the lower face
  • Desire for a more youthful neck appearance

Ideal candidates are in good overall health, non-smokers, and have realistic expectations about the results. During your consultation, Dr. Jonathan Kramer will evaluate your neck anatomy and skin quality to determine the best approach for your goals.

The Consultation Process

Your neck lift journey begins with a comprehensive consultation with Dr. Jonathan Kramer. During this appointment, you will:

  • Discuss your concerns and aesthetic goals
  • Review your medical history and current health
  • Undergo a physical examination of your neck and face
  • Learn about the surgical techniques recommended for you
  • View before and after photos of similar patients
  • Discuss whether combining with a facelift would benefit you
  • Receive a personalized treatment plan

Surgical Technique

Dr. Jonathan Kramer customizes each neck lift procedure based on the patient's unique anatomy and goals. The surgery typically involves:

Incision Placement

Incisions are carefully placed to minimize visible scarring:

  • Behind the ears: The primary incisions extend behind the ears and into the hairline
  • Under the chin: A small incision may be made under the chin if liposuction or direct muscle repair is needed

Techniques Used

  • Platysmaplasty: Tightening of the platysma muscle to eliminate bands
  • Liposuction: Removal of excess fat deposits under the chin
  • Skin removal: Excision of excess skin for a smooth contour
  • SMAS tightening: Deep tissue repositioning for longer-lasting results

Recovery & Results

After surgery, you'll wear a compression garment to minimize swelling and support healing. Most patients experience moderate swelling and bruising for the first 2 weeks.

Recovery Timeline:

  • Week 1: Rest at home, keep head elevated, wear compression garment
  • Week 2: Most bruising resolves, return to light activities
  • Week 3-4: Return to work and most normal activities
  • Week 6: Resume exercise and strenuous activities

Results are immediately visible once swelling subsides, with continued improvement over 3-6 months. Neck lift results typically last 10-15 years.
