Brow Lift

Home / Face / Brow Lift

Lift and Rejuvenate Your Brow in Boise

A brow lift, also known as a forehead lift, elevates sagging eyebrows, smooths forehead wrinkles, and reduces frown lines to create a more youthful, refreshed appearance. Dr. Jonathan Kramer uses advanced techniques to achieve natural-looking results that open up your eyes and brighten your entire face.

As we age, the brow naturally descends, causing a tired, angry, or sad appearance even when we feel vibrant and happy. Deep forehead creases and frown lines can make you look older than you feel. A brow lift addresses these concerns by repositioning the brow to a more youthful position and smoothing the forehead.

Benefits of Brow Lift Surgery

  • Elevation of sagging, drooping eyebrows
  • Reduction of horizontal forehead creases
  • Softening of frown lines between the eyebrows
  • More open, alert, and youthful eye appearance
  • Improved facial symmetry
  • Natural-looking rejuvenation
  • Long-lasting results (10-12 years)

Brow Lift Techniques

Dr. Jonathan Kramer offers several brow lift techniques, selecting the best approach based on your anatomy and goals:

Endoscopic Brow Lift

The most common technique, using small incisions hidden within the hairline and a tiny camera to guide the procedure. This approach offers minimal scarring, shorter recovery, and natural results.

Temporal (Limited Incision) Brow Lift

Ideal for patients who need lifting primarily in the outer brow area. Small incisions are made within the hairline at the temples to lift the lateral brow.

Coronal Brow Lift

A traditional technique using a longer incision from ear to ear across the top of the head, hidden within the hair. Best for patients needing significant lifting or those with very deep forehead creases.

Am I a Candidate for a Brow Lift?

You may be an ideal candidate for a brow lift if you have:

  • Sagging or drooping eyebrows
  • Deep horizontal forehead creases
  • Frown lines or furrows between the eyebrows
  • A tired, sad, or angry appearance due to brow position
  • Excess skin or tissue hanging over the upper eyelids
  • Asymmetrical eyebrows

Ideal candidates are in good overall health, non-smokers, and have realistic expectations about the results.

The Consultation Process

Your brow lift journey begins with a comprehensive consultation. During this appointment, you will:

  • Discuss your concerns and aesthetic goals
  • Review your medical history and current health
  • Undergo an examination of your brow, forehead, and upper face
  • Learn about the surgical technique recommended for you
  • View before and after photos of similar patients
  • Discuss whether combining with eyelid surgery would benefit you

Recovery & Results

After surgery, you may experience some swelling, bruising, and temporary numbness in the forehead area. Most patients feel comfortable returning to public activities within 2 weeks.

Recovery Timeline:

  • Week 1: Rest at home, keep head elevated, manage swelling with cold compresses
  • Week 2: Most sutures/staples removed, bruising fading
  • Week 2-3: Return to work and most normal activities
  • Week 4-6: Resume exercise and strenuous activities

Results are visible immediately and continue to improve as swelling resolves over 2-3 months. Brow lift results typically last 10-12 years.
