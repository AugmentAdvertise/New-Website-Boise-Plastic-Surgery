Expert Facelift Surgery in Scottsdale

A facelift, medically known as rhytidectomy, is a transformative facial rejuvenation procedure that addresses the visible signs of aging in the face and neck. Dr. Aldo Guerra specializes in advanced facelift techniques that deliver natural, refreshed results while preserving your unique facial characteristics.

As we age, the effects of gravity, sun exposure, and the natural loss of facial volume lead to sagging skin, deep creases, jowls, and loose skin along the jawline and neck. A facelift effectively reverses these changes, restoring a more youthful facial contour without creating an overly tight or unnatural appearance.

Benefits of Facelift Surgery

  • Eliminates jowls and restores a defined jawline
  • Smooths deep creases and folds around the nose and mouth
  • Tightens loose, sagging skin in the lower face and neck
  • Restores youthful facial contours and proportions
  • Provides long-lasting anti-aging results (10-15 years)
  • Boosts self-confidence and refreshes your overall appearance

Types of Facelift Procedures

Dr. Aldo Guerra offers several facelift techniques to address varying degrees of facial aging. During your consultation, he will recommend the approach best suited to your anatomy and goals.

Full Facelift (Traditional Facelift)

A full facelift provides comprehensive rejuvenation for patients with moderate to severe signs of aging. This technique addresses the entire face, including the midface, lower face, jowls, and neck. Incisions are carefully placed along the hairline and around the ears, allowing Dr. Aldo Guerra to lift and reposition the underlying facial muscles and tissues, remove excess skin, and create natural, long-lasting results.

Mini Facelift

The mini facelift is ideal for patients with early to moderate signs of aging who want significant improvement with less extensive surgery. Using smaller incisions and a shorter recovery period, the mini facelift targets the lower face and jowls, providing a noticeable lift and rejuvenation. This technique is often combined with other procedures such as neck liposuction for enhanced results.

Deep Plane Facelift

The deep plane facelift is an advanced technique that lifts the deeper layers of facial tissue, including the SMAS (superficial musculoaponeurotic system) and underlying muscles, as a single unit. This approach provides more natural movement and longer-lasting results compared to traditional techniques. The deep plane facelift is particularly effective for addressing midface descent, nasolabial folds, and significant jowling.

SMAS Facelift

The SMAS facelift specifically targets the muscular layer beneath the skin, tightening and repositioning these tissues separately from the skin. This creates a more natural result with less tension on the skin itself, reducing the risk of a pulled or unnatural appearance.

Ideal Candidates for Facelift Surgery

The best candidates for a facelift typically share the following characteristics:

  • Age 40-70 with visible signs of facial aging
  • Sagging skin in the midface and/or lower face
  • Deep creases below the lower eyelids or along the nose
  • Loss of muscle tone and fatty deposits in the face
  • Jowls or loss of definition along the jawline
  • Loose skin and excess fat under the chin and jaw
  • Good overall health and non-smoker (or willing to quit)
  • Realistic expectations about outcomes

The Facelift Procedure

Facelift surgery is performed under general anesthesia or intravenous sedation and typically takes 3-5 hours depending on the extent of the procedure. Dr. Aldo Guerra uses meticulous surgical techniques to achieve optimal results:

  • Incision Placement: Incisions begin at the temples, continue around the ear, and extend into the hairline behind the ear. For patients who need neck improvement, a small incision may be made under the chin.
  • Tissue Repositioning: The underlying facial muscles and tissues (SMAS layer) are lifted and repositioned to restore youthful contours.
  • Skin Redraping: The skin is gently redraped over the newly elevated tissues, and excess skin is removed.
  • Incision Closure: Incisions are carefully closed with sutures designed to minimize scarring and promote optimal healing.

Recovery & Results

Understanding the recovery process helps ensure you achieve the best possible results:

  • First Week: Expect swelling and bruising, which is normal. A compression garment may be worn to minimize swelling. Most sutures are removed within 5-7 days.
  • Weeks 2-3: Swelling and bruising significantly subside. Many patients feel comfortable returning to work and light social activities.
  • Months 1-3: Residual swelling continues to improve. You'll see progressive improvement in your results as tissues settle into their new position.
  • Final Results: Full results are typically visible at 3-6 months, revealing a refreshed, more youthful appearance that looks natural and harmonious.

Dr. Aldo Guerra provides detailed post-operative instructions and is available throughout your recovery to ensure optimal healing and results.

Combining Procedures for Enhanced Results

Many patients choose to combine their facelift with complementary procedures to achieve comprehensive facial rejuvenation:

  • Neck Lift: Addresses loose skin, muscle banding, and excess fat in the neck
  • Brow Lift: Elevates drooping brows and smooths forehead wrinkles
  • Eyelid Surgery: Rejuvenates tired-looking eyes by removing excess skin and fat
  • Fat Transfer: Restores lost facial volume for a more youthful fullness
  • Skin Resurfacing: Improves skin texture, tone, and fine lines
