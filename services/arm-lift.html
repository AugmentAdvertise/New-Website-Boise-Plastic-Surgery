Arm Lift

Home / Body / Arm Lift

Achieve Toned, Sculpted Arms with Brachioplasty in Scottsdale

An arm lift, or brachioplasty, removes excess skin and fat from the upper arms to create a more toned, youthful appearance. Dr. Aldo Guerra uses precise surgical techniques to eliminate "bat wings" and give you arms you'll be proud to show off.

Loose, sagging skin on the upper arms is a common concern, particularly after weight loss or as a natural part of aging. Despite regular exercise and strength training, this excess skin cannot be tightened through non-surgical means. An arm lift provides the solution, removing the excess skin and creating smooth, contoured arms.

Benefits of Arm Lift Surgery

  • Elimination of excess, hanging skin ("bat wings")
  • More toned, defined arm contour
  • Improved arm proportions
  • Greater confidence in sleeveless clothing
  • Reduced skin irritation and chafing
  • Long-lasting results

Types of Arm Lifts

Traditional Arm Lift

The most common approach, involving an incision from the armpit to the elbow along the inner arm. This technique allows for maximum skin removal and is ideal for patients with significant skin laxity.

Mini Arm Lift

For patients with mild to moderate skin laxity concentrated near the armpit, a mini arm lift involves a smaller incision hidden in the armpit crease. Recovery is quicker, but results are more limited.

Extended Arm Lift

For patients who also have excess skin extending onto the side of the chest, an extended arm lift continues the incision onto the lateral chest wall for comprehensive contouring.

Am I a Candidate for an Arm Lift?

You may be an ideal candidate for an arm lift if you have:

  • Loose, sagging skin on the upper arms
  • "Bat wings" that don't improve with exercise
  • Excess skin after significant weight loss
  • Age-related skin laxity in the arms
  • Difficulty finding clothing that fits your arms
  • Self-consciousness about your arm appearance

Ideal candidates are at a stable weight, non-smokers, and have realistic expectations. During your consultation, Dr. Aldo Guerra will evaluate your skin quality and recommend the best approach.

The Consultation Process

Your arm lift journey begins with a comprehensive consultation. During this appointment, you will:

  • Discuss your concerns and aesthetic goals
  • Review your medical history and weight loss history
  • Undergo an examination of your arms
  • Learn about the surgical technique recommended for you
  • View before and after photos of similar patients
  • Discuss scarring and recovery expectations

Recovery & Results

After surgery, your arms will be wrapped in compression garments to minimize swelling and support healing. Drains may be placed temporarily to prevent fluid accumulation.

Recovery Timeline:

  • Week 1: Rest, keep arms elevated when possible, wear compression garments
  • Week 2: Return to desk work, light activities
  • Week 3-4: Resume most normal activities
  • Week 6: Resume exercise and lifting

Results are immediately visible and continue to improve as swelling subsides over 3-6 months. Arm lift results are long-lasting, though aging and weight fluctuations can affect outcomes over time.
