Welcome to Kramer Plastic Surgery

Boise's Trusted Plastic Surgeon

Dr. Jonathan Kramer is a board-certified plastic surgeon dedicated to helping patients in Boise and the Treasure Valley achieve their personal goals through beautiful transformations.

He attended Vanderbilt University, earned his Medical Degree at Emory University, completed a five-year general surgery residency where he was awarded Outstanding Surgical Intern, and finished a two-year plastic surgery fellowship at the University of Colorado Health Sciences Center in Denver.
